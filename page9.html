VisiScope Spinning Disk

Spinning Disk Confocal Microscope

Real-Time Confocal for wide field of view, improved image quality and high speed live cell imaging . The VisiScope-Real-Time Confocal System based on Yokogawa CSU-W1 scan head.

CSU-W1 system  has the widest field of view in the industry providing 4X wider FOV than conventional scanners.

VisiScope is equipped with dual Andor’s Zyla 4.2PLUS sCMOS camera platform which offers high speed, high sensitivity and high resolution imaging performance
