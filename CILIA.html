Spectral detectors

In a spectral detector system, light is divided into multiple spectral bands (3 in Leica, 2 in our Olympus confocal). The bands are independent and continuously tunable within the full visible spectrum. The light in each band is detected by a light sensor. This setup allows fluorescence emission ranges to be freely adjustable. 

More info


AOBS

AOBS (Acousto Optical Beam Splitter) uses a crystal instead of fixed wavelength mirrors to split the excitation from emission light. This way, AOBS can better separate close colors like GFP from YFP. We have this AOBS technology only on our Leica confocal microscope!
