AOBS
We have AOBS (Acousto Optical Beam Splitter) technology only on our Leica confocal. It uses a crystal instead of fixed wavelength mirrors to split the excitation from emission light. This way, AOBS can better separate close colors like GFP from YFP.
A non-AOBS confocal (like our Olympus), needs a dedicated glass mirror (beam splitter) to separate e.g. GFP from YFP, With Leica AOBS, we can separate many different close colors without having a dedicated beam splitting mirror!
