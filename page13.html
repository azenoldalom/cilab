2 Photon microscope

Femtonics FEMTO2D Alba two-photon microscope

Two-photon microscopy uses intense pulsed infrared laser to image deep into biological sample. It can be used for imaging thick tissue specimens or even imaging deep inside live animals.

Note: This microscope is an instrument of the Institute of Biophysics operated by the Neurovascular Unit Research Group. Please contact Dr. István Krizbai if you would like to use this microscope. 

Properties of  two photon microscope

Lasers

• Mai Tai HP Ti-sapphire laser


Objectives

20x water
XLUMPLFLN-20XW
60x water
LUMFLN60XW
Electrophysiology objectives


Detection

• GaAsP photomultipliers

Unique features!

• Deep tissue imaging
• Upright microscope for live animal imaging
• Multiple Line-Scan mode
• Folded frame scanning
• Real-time data analysis
• High speed digitally controlled galvanometer optical scanners
