Olympus Stereo

Olympus SZX12 Fluorescence Stereozoom Microscope

User manual Ins. Manual

The SZX microscope system is the ultimate research stereo microscope in the Olympus line. Long working distance, wide zoom range, excellent image clarity and easy operation are matched by a modular system of components giving the SZX great flexibility, allowing adaptation to meet the needs of virtually any stereo microscope user.

The SZX gives the user maximum flexibility in observing the specimen without switching objectives. Two objectives are available along with a dual nosepiece. This allows the user to utilize the full zoom range of two objectives by easily rotating one or the other into the light path. Using the 0.5x and 1.0x objectives and 10x eyepieces on the SZX12 gives an effective zoom range of 3.5x to 90x, or a 26:1 ratio.

Ringlight LED System

Our Stereozoom microscope has a Schott EasyLED Ringlight ergonomic illumination system. The EasyLED series is an innovative illumination system specially designed for stereo microscopy. The ringlight system integrates high brightness LEDs and controller electronics into the head of the illuminator. This saves space on the workbench and allows easy and ergonomic operation, directly on the microscope. The system incorporates 45 high brightness LEDs with continuous dimming (0-100%) and maximum illuminance of 90klx at 75mm free working distance.
