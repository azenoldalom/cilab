Olympus FV1000

Laser Scanning Confocal Microscope

This confocal microscope is ideal for 2D or 3D visualization and quantification of specimens ranging from single cells and tissues to small organisms. The FV1000 is capable of simultaneous and sequential high-resolution imaging of three or more fluorophores. Differential Interference Contrast (DIC) is also available to acquire a high contrast image of a transparent sample. 

The FV1000 system supports state-of-the-art imaging applications including colocalization, volume rendering, particle tracking and motion analysis, fluorescence recovery after photobleaching (FRAP), fluorescence loss in photobleaching (FLIP), and fluorescence resonance energy transfer (FRET).
