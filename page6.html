Leica SP5

Laser Scanning Confocal Microscope

The Leica TCS SP5 Confocal  fully covers a broad range of requirements in confocal imaging with excellent overall performance. The system provides wide range of scan speeds and up to 8192 x 8192 pixel resolution.

With its high-efficiency spectral detection (three channels simultaneously) and AOBS (Acousto Optical Bream Splitter), the Leica TCS SP5 delivers bright, noise-free images with minimal photo damage at high speed. 

The SP5 system supports state-of-the-art imaging applications including colocalization, fluorescence recovery after photobleaching (FRAP), fluorescence loss in photobleaching (FLIP), and fluorescence resonance energy transfer (FRET).
